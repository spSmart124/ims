# Ideation
Think about creating a systems in below sequence.
1. Infinite
1. Large
1. Medium
1. Small
1. Specific

## Infinite
Infinite system should be applicabe to the world which means it should be international. It may also be designed to be applicable to the universe, beyond earth to extra terestrial systems.

## Large
Think about different large systems under an Infinite system which should be applicable to at least to a country and may go beyond to be applicable to the whole world.

## Medium
Medium systems come under a large system. These are components of a large system.

## Small
Small systems come under a medium system. These are constituents of a medium system. They are applicable for small countable set of subjects.

## Specific
A specific system is applicable to a small system which is designed specifically to work for that system. It may or may not be applicable for other small systems.
